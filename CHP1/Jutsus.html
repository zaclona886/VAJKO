Amaterasu

Amaterasu is highest level of Fire Release.Amaterasu produces black flames at the focal point of the user's vision. In most situations, this makes Amaterasu impossible to avoid. However, techniques of extreme speed allow the avoidance or interception of the jutsu after it has been cast.Once created, the flames will not stop burning until their target is completely incinerated
